Our Specialized Services

Comprehensive Care for Neurological and Physical Conditions

We provide specialized treatment for a range of conditions, focusing on personalized care plans that address your specific needs and goals for recovery.

Paralysis Rehabilitation

Comprehensive rehabilitation programs designed to improve mobility, strength, and quality of life for patients with paralysis.

Cerebral Palsy Therapy

Specialized therapy techniques that focus on improving motor skills, coordination, and overall function for cerebral palsy patients.

Cervical Pain Treatment

Advanced treatments that target the root causes of cervical pain to provide lasting relief and improved neck mobility.

Frozen Shoulder Recovery

Effective therapeutic approaches to restore range of motion and alleviate pain associated with frozen shoulder conditions.

Sciatica Pain Management

Targeted interventions to reduce sciatic nerve inflammation and provide relief from radiating pain down the lower back and legs.
